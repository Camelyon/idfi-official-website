ABOUT & TOKENS

IDFI is a liquidity-first DeFi ecosystem built around soft-pegged assets and a transparent LP engine. Peg tokens such as IDOLL and IDEU track major currencies, while IDFI is the governance and revenue-sharing token of the protocol.

IDOLL

Independent Dollar

IDOLL is a soft-pegged USD token designed for on-chain liquidity rather than strict 1:1 stability. It tracks the value of the dollar within a flexible band and is primarily used in IDFI liquidity pools on BSC and Solana. IDOLL is the main dollar leg of the ecosystem’s LP and market-making strategies.

IDEU

Independent Euro

IDEU is a Euro-referenced soft-pegged asset that gives DeFi users simple EUR exposure without complex collateral or borrowing. It is designed to sit in a target price range around the Euro while staying highly liquid in AMM pools. Together with IDOLL, IDEU forms the core FX pair of the IDFI ecosystem.

IDFI

Independent Digital Finance Token

IDFI is the governance and revenue-sharing token of the ecosystem. It captures fees and LP rewards generated by IDFI liquidity pools and automated market-making strategies. Over time, active contributors, LP providers and strategic partners will be able to earn IDFI based on transparent, on-chain metrics.
