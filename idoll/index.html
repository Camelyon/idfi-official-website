IDOLL — Independent Dollar

IDOLL is a BNB Smart Chain soft-pegged digital dollar asset within the Independent Digital Finance ecosystem. It is designed for transparent on-chain liquidity, live AMM market activity and measured liquidity-pool growth.

Supply Profile

IDOLL uses an uncapped maximum supply model with operational minting controls. The contract is configured with a daily minting limit of 100,000 IDOLL. Current supply figures should always be verified on-chain.

Initial documented supply: 200,000 IDOLL

Current circulation supply: 250,000 IDOLL

Liquidity Model

IDOLL liquidity is deployed on PancakeSwap V3 through defined LP ranges. The project uses gradual LP additions, live market monitoring and public analytics to support transparent market development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is IDOLL traded?

The active IDOLL / USDT market is live on PancakeSwap V3 on BNB Smart Chain.

How can market activity be verified?

Users can verify IDOLL market data through public analytics platforms such as DexScreener, GeckoTerminal, DexTools and BscScan.

Is IDOLL a guaranteed redeemable dollar?

No. IDOLL is a soft-pegged on-chain asset and does not represent a bank deposit or guaranteed redemption claim. Market price can move above or below its reference value.

What is the daily mint limit of IDOLL?

The IDOLL contract includes an operational daily mint limit of 100,000 IDOLL. Current supply information should always be verified on-chain.

How can users verify IDOLL information?

Users can verify IDOLL through public blockchain explorers and analytics services including BscScan, DexScreener, GeckoTerminal and other public market-data providers.

Is IDOLL planned to expand beyond BNB Smart Chain?

Yes. The current focus is BNB Smart Chain infrastructure and liquidity growth. Future ecosystem development may include Solana expansion and additional liquidity markets as the project matures.

Does IDFI plan to pursue centralized exchange listings?

The project may evaluate centralized exchange opportunities in the future. Current priorities focus on documentation, market visibility, public analytics platforms and sustainable liquidity growth.

What comes after IDOLL?

The long-term ecosystem roadmap includes IDEU (Independent Euro), additional liquidity infrastructure and the future IDFI governance token.
